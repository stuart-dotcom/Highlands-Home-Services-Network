Privacy Policy — Highlands Home Services Network

This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information submitted through this website by Highlands County homeowners and licensed contractor applicants.

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Highlands Home Services Network ("we," "us," or "our") operates highlandshomeservicesnetwork.com. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information submitted through this website. Highlands Home Services Network is a digital lead generation and referral service — a registered DBA of Obsidian Peak Holdings LLC, a Florida Limited Liability Company. We are not a licensed contractor and do not perform trade services.

Information we collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide through our contractor application and homeowner service request forms, including: name, business name, phone number, email address, Florida license number, service area details, and home service request information. We do not collect payment information directly — no credit card or billing data is transmitted through this site.

How we use your information

Information submitted through our forms is used solely to facilitate local home service connections in Highlands County, Florida (Sebring, Avon Park, Lake Placid, Lorida, and Venus).

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third-party data brokers or marketing agencies. Homeowner information is shared only with the single, specific licensed contractor assigned to your service request. It is never sold or distributed to any other party. Contractor applicant information is used solely to evaluate network fit and verify Florida DBPR licensure.

Analytics and cookies

This website may use basic analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand aggregate traffic patterns. These tools use cookies that collect anonymized data about site usage. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time without affecting your ability to use this site.

Data security and retention

This site is served over HTTPS. All form submissions are transmitted securely to our form processing provider. We retain application and service request data only as long as necessary for legitimate business operations and do not maintain long-term databases of personal records.

Your rights (Florida FDUTPA & CCPA)

Regardless of where you are located, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time. California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to request deletion. Florida residents are protected under the Florida Deceptive and Unfair Trade Practices Act (FDUTPA). To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

Third-party links

This site contains links to external websites including Florida DBPR, Sunbiz.org, and Google Business Profile. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of any third-party site. We recommend reviewing their policies independently.
