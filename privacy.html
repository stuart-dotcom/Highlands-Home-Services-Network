Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Highlands Home Services Network ("we," "us," or "our") operates highlandshomeservicesnetwork.com. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information submitted through this website.

Information we collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide through our contractor application and contact forms: name, business name, phone number, email address, Florida license number, and service area details. For homeowners, we collect name, phone number, email address, service address, and details about your home service request.

How we use your information

Information submitted through our homeowner service requests or contractor application forms is used solely to facilitate local home service connections.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third-party data brokers or marketing agencies. Homeowner information is only shared with the single, specific licensed contractor assigned to your service request. It is never sold or distributed to any other party.

Analytics and cookies

This website may use basic analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand aggregate traffic patterns. These tools use cookies that collect anonymized data. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Data security and retention

This site is served over HTTPS. Form submissions are transmitted securely to our form processing provider. We retain application data only as long as necessary for business operations.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at hello@highlandshomeservicesnetwork.com.
