Common questions

What contractors ask before they apply.

What is an exclusive contractor lead?

An exclusive lead means the homeowner's contact information and job request are sent to only one contractor in our network — never shared with competing companies. You are the sole contractor who receives that opportunity.

How does DBPR license verification work?

Before your account is activated, your Florida trade license number is verified against the Florida Department of Business and Professional Regulation (DBPR) database at myfloridalicense.com. Only contractors with an active, valid license are approved.

Does the network cover all of Highlands County?

Yes. Highlands Home Services Network serves the entire county — Sebring, Avon Park, Lake Placid, Lorida, and Venus. Operations remain within Highlands County, Florida only.

Are there long-term contracts or monthly fees?

No. The network operates on a pay-per-lead model. You pay only for verified leads delivered. There are no monthly retainers, no ad spend requirements, and no cancellation penalties.

Which trades are currently accepting contractors in Highlands County?

HVAC contractors serving Sebring, Avon Park, and Lake Placid are currently being accepted. Plumbing, electrical, and roofing verticals are in pre-launch with waitlists open. Each trade vertical accepts one licensed contractor per primary service zone.
