Apply to join the Highlands Home Services Network.

To join the Highlands Home Services Network, a contractor must hold an active Florida trade license verifiable via the DBPR database, physically serve homeowners in Highlands County — Sebring, Avon Park, Lake Placid, Lorida, or Venus — and pass a quality vetting review. Applications are reviewed within 1 business day. The network operates on a pay-per-lead basis with no monthly fees and no long-term contracts.

We are building a tightly vetted network of local professionals in Highlands County. To join, you must meet three requirements:

  • Active Florida license: You must hold a valid trade license verifiable via the Florida DBPR.
  • Local service area: You must physically serve homeowners in Highlands County — Sebring, Avon Park, Lake Placid, Lorida, or Venus.
  • Commitment to quality: You must contact exclusive homeowner leads promptly and professionally.
