How your request works.

  1. Submit your issue: Fill out the secure form below with your home service problem and urgency level.
  2. Local verification: We review your request to make sure you are within our Highlands County service area (Sebring, Avon Park, Lake Placid, Lorida, or Venus).
  3. Direct connection: Your request is routed to a DBPR-verified, licensed contractor who covers your city and trade.
  4. Fast response: The contractor contacts you via your preferred method — usually within the hour for emergencies. There is zero obligation to hire.

What we are (and aren’t).

Highlands Home Services Network is a free digital referral service, not a home services company. We do not perform trade work, dispatch trucks, or charge homeowners. Our sole purpose is to connect you with an independent, DBPR-licensed contractor who can help with your project. You always have the final say on whether to hire them after receiving their pricing.

Highlands Home Services Network is a referral and lead generation service. We do not perform HVAC or any other trade services.
