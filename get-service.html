Request HVAC service from a licensed Highlands County contractor.

Submit a free service request below. A licensed Highlands County HVAC contractor will contact you directly — usually within the hour for urgent issues.

How your request works.

Your submission is reviewed to confirm your location is within our Highlands County service area.

Your request is then sent to a DBPR-verified HVAC contractor who covers your city. We serve Sebring, Avon Park, Lake Placid, Lorida, Venus, and nearby communities in Highlands County.

You’ll hear from them by your preferred method — usually within the hour for urgent requests, or at your requested time for non-emergencies.

There is no obligation to hire. The referral is completely free to you as a homeowner.

What we are (and aren’t).

Highlands Home Services Network is a digital lead generation and referral service. We do not perform HVAC work ourselves.

We only connect you with licensed, DBPR-verified contractors. You choose whether to move forward with the contractor after they contact you and provide pricing.
