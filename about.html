About Highlands Home Services Network

Local, Highlands County–only contractor lead generation.

We’re not a national franchise. We’re not a software platform from out of state. We’re a local operation focused on one county, built for licensed contractors who call Highlands County home.

Why we built Highlands Home Services Network.

Highlands County contractors are underserved by the big lead generation platforms. Services like HomeAdvisor and Angi share the same lead with multiple contractors, charge for leads regardless of outcome, and have no local accountability.

We built Highlands Home Services Network to fix that — one county, one trade vertical at a time, with exclusive delivery and a straightforward pay-per-lead model.

We operate homeowner-facing digital properties targeting Highlands County residents searching for local home service contractors. Every lead that enters our system is reviewed before it’s delivered to a contractor.

Who we are (and what we are not).

Highlands Home Services Network is a DBA of Obsidian Peak Holdings LLC, a Florida-registered company. We are a digital lead generation and referral service — not a licensed contractor and not a marketplace that sells the same lead five times.

We verify every contractor’s Florida license against the DBPR database before activating their account. We do not activate unlicensed applicants. We do not share leads. We do not perform any trade services ourselves.

Our role is simple: find Highlands County homeowners who need a licensed tradesperson, screen their requests, and connect them with one contractor in our network who is equipped to do the work.

Highlands County, Florida — by design.

We serve Highlands County, Florida only — Sebring, Avon Park, Lake Placid, Lorida, and Venus. This isn’t a scalability limitation — it’s a deliberate choice.

Hyperlocal focus means better lead quality, faster homeowner response times, and stronger contractor relationships than any national platform can offer in a market this size.

By committing to a single county, we can invest in deeper coverage across multiple trades while keeping service areas tight and reducing wasted miles for contractors.
