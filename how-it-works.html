The process

The 6-Step Path from Homeowner Request to Your Phone

Every lead we deliver passes through every one of these steps — which is exactly why our leads are different from what you find on the big platforms.

  1. 1

    Apply & Get Verified — Join a Network Homeowners Trust

    Submit your application with your Florida trade license number and service areas. We verify every applicant against the Florida DBPR database ↗ before approving your account. This protects the homeowners who rely on our referrals — and protects your standing among peers who cleared the same bar.

  2. 2

    Hyper-Local Targeting Catches the Homeowner at the Right Moment

    We manage high-traffic digital properties that attract Highlands County residents in Sebring, Avon Park, Lake Placid, Lorida, and Venus who are actively searching for your trade right now. These are not cold audiences — they arrived with a specific, current need.

  3. 3

    Every Lead Passes a Rigorous Screen — If It Fails, You Never See It

    Every inquiry is screened for a confirmed Highlands County address, validated contact details, and genuine service intent. If a submission does not meet our standards, it is permanently discarded — never sold to you or to anyone else. You only receive leads that have cleared every checkpoint.

  4. 4

    Your Territory Gets Locked — No Other Contractor Touches It

    Once a lead passes screening, it is matched exclusively to you based on your service area. That homeowner's name, address, phone, and email are yours and yours alone. No other contractor in the network will ever see it — not now, not later.

  5. 5

    Real-Time Delivery — Be the First Voice They Hear

    Leads are delivered instantly via SMS, email, or phone based on your preference. For emergency service requests, we trigger an immediate phone bridge so you are the first voice the homeowner hears — before they have a chance to look anywhere else.

  6. 6

    Invoice the Homeowner Directly — We Invoice You for the Lead

    You invoice the homeowner directly and keep 100% of your margin. We invoice you only for verified leads delivered at your agreed per-lead rate — after delivery, never in advance. A lead is considered delivered and billable when verified contact information and a confirmed service request are transmitted to you, regardless of whether the job is quoted, scheduled, or completed.

🛡 Our Lead Quality Commitment

We stand behind every lead we deliver. If a lead fails our own verification standards after delivery, we make it right.

What qualifies for a credit review:

  • Invalid contact information — phone disconnected, email bounced, or submission undeliverable. Submit a credit request within 48 hours with documentation and we will review and credit if confirmed.
  • Address outside our Highlands County service area — submit within 48 hours. We will verify against our own records. If confirmed out-of-area, we will credit the lead.
  • Duplicate lead — same homeowner delivered to you twice. We will credit immediately, no review required.

What is not eligible for a credit:

  • Homeowner requested a quote but did not hire
  • No response to your follow-up attempts
  • You were unavailable or missed the delivery window
  • Pricing or scope disagreement with the homeowner

Inquiries that fail our screening are permanently discarded — never sold to you, another contractor, or any third party. This policy is stated at setup and reflected in your contractor agreement.
