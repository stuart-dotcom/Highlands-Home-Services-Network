The process

From sourcing homeowner leads to getting them on your phone.

Here is exactly what happens at every step.

What we do — and what we don't.

Highlands Home Services Network is not a contractor. We are a digital lead generation and referral service. Our job is to find local homeowners who need a licensed tradesperson — and connect them with the right contractor in our network.

We operate homeowner-facing digital properties targeting Highlands County residents. When a homeowner submits a service request, we review it, confirm it meets our quality standards, and route it exclusively to one network contractor.

Highlands Home Services Network is a referral and lead generation service. We do not perform trade services.

What that means for you.

  • You never compete on the same lead.
  • You never pay for tire-kickers.
  • You are never locked into a contract.

The six steps: application to lead delivery.

  1. 1

    Application & Credential Review

    You submit your application with your Florida trade license number and cities covered. We verify your license against the Florida DBPR database ↗ before approving your account.

  2. 2

    Where Leads Come From

    We operate homeowner-facing digital properties targeting Highlands County residents searching for home services — Sebring, Avon Park, Lake Placid, Lorida, and Venus.

  3. 3

    Lead Screening & Quality Review

    Every inquiry is screened for confirmed Highlands County location, genuine service intent, and valid contact information. Submissions that do not qualify are discarded — never sold.

  4. 4

    Exclusive Routing to One Contractor

    Once a lead passes screening it is matched to the single network contractor whose service area covers that homeowner. Never sent to multiple contractors. Full homeowner contact info included.

  5. 5

    Lead Delivery

    Delivered in real time via phone, SMS, or email — your preference. Emergency leads trigger an immediate call in addition to digital delivery.

  6. 6

    You Close the Job — We Invoice for the Lead

    You invoice the homeowner directly. We invoice you only for verified leads delivered at the pay-per-lead rate agreed at setup. No monthly fees, no retainers, no long-term contracts.

What is included in every lead.

  • Verified service area: We confirm the homeowner's address is within our active Highlands County coverage before routing.
  • Genuine intent: We filter out price shoppers. The homeowners we send to you have a genuine, current need.
  • Full contact info: Name, address, phone, and email — everything you need to follow up immediately.
  • Exclusive delivery: Once routed to you, no other contractor in the network receives that homeowner's information.
