The process

The 6-Step Path from Homeowner Request to Your Phone

Every lead we deliver to you passes through every one of these steps. This is why our leads are different from what you find on the big platforms.

  1. 1

    Apply & Get Verified

    Submit your application with your Florida trade license number and service areas. We verify every applicant against the Florida DBPR database ↗ before approving your account. This keeps our network trusted by homeowners — and keeps out anyone who cuts corners.

  2. 2

    Local Homeowners Find Us

    We manage high-traffic digital properties that attract Highlands County residents who are actively searching for home services in Sebring, Avon Park, Lake Placid, Lorida, and Venus. These are not cold audiences — they arrived looking for help with a specific trade need.

  3. 3

    We Screen Every Lead — Rigorously

    You only pay for quality. We screen every inquiry for a confirmed Highlands County address, valid contact details, and genuine service intent. If a submission doesn't meet our standards, we discard it — we never sell it to you or anyone else.

  4. 4

    You Get 100% Exclusive Leads

    Unlike the big platforms, we never send a lead to multiple contractors. Once a request passes screening, it is matched exclusively to you based on your service area. That homeowner's name, address, phone, and email are yours and yours alone — no other contractor in the network will ever see it.

  5. 5

    Real-Time Delivery — Speed Wins the Job

    Leads are delivered instantly via phone, SMS, or email based on your preference. For emergency service requests, we trigger an immediate phone call so you can respond before the homeowner has a chance to search elsewhere.

  6. 6

    Close the Job — Then Pay

    You invoice the homeowner directly and keep 100% of the profit. We only invoice you for verified leads delivered at your agreed per-lead rate. No retainers. No hidden fees. No long-term contracts. If you stop getting value, you can stop at any time.

  • You only pay for what you get. Billing happens after leads are delivered and confirmed, never in advance.
